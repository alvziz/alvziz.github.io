Recipe #1: Homemade Pizza

  • Time: Prep: 25 min. + rising
    Bake: 25 min.
  • Serves: 2 pizzas (3 servings each)
Ingredients Quantity
dry yeast 1/4 ounce
warm water 1/4 cups
canola/olive oil 1/4 cup
all-purpose flour 3-1/2 to 4 cups
tomato sauce 1 can (15 ounces)
dried oregano 3 teaspoons
dried basil 1 teaspoon
part-skim mozzarella cheese 2 cups shredded

Step 1: In large bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in water; let stand for 5 minutes. Add oil and salt. Stir in flour, 1 cup at a time, until a soft dough forms.

Step 2: Turn onto a floured surface; knead until smooth and elastic, 2-3 minutes. Place in a greased bowl, turning once to grease the top. Cover and let rise in a warm place until doubled, about 45 minutes.

Step 3: Meanwhile, cook beef and onion over medium heat until beef is no longer pink, breaking meat into crumbles; drain.

Step 4: Punch down dough; divide in half. Press each half into a greased 12-in. pizza pan. Combine the tomato sauce, oregano and basil; spread over each crust. Top with beef mixture, green pepper and cheese.

Step 5: Bake at 400Â° for 25-30 minutes or until crust is lightly browned.

Recipe #2: Scrambled Eggs

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 3 servings
Ingredients Quantity
large eggs 6
cup evaporated milk or half-and-half cream 1/4 cup
salt 1/4 teaspoon
pepper 1/8 teaspoon
canola oil 1 tablespoon
process cheese sauce 2 tablespoons

Step 1: In a bowl, whisk eggs, milk, salt and pepper until the mixture is blended.

Step 2: In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Pour in egg mixture; stir in cheese sauce.

Step 3: Cook and stir until eggs are thickened and no liquid egg remains.

Mac & cheese recipe

  • Time: 50 mins
  • Serves: 4 servings
Ingredients Quantity
uncooked elbow macaroni 8 ounces
shredded sharp Cheddar cheese 2 cups
Parmesan cheese 1/2 cup
milk 3 cups
butter 1/4 cup
all-purpose flour 2 1/2 tablespoons
butter 2 tablespoons
breadcrumbs 1/2 cup
paprika 1 pinch

Step 1: Cook macaroni according to the package directions. Drain.

Step 2: In a saucepan, melt butter or margarine over medium heat. Stir in enough flour to make a roux. Add milk to roux slowly, stirring constantly. Stir in cheeses, and cook over low heat until cheese is melted and the sauce is a little thick.

Step 3: Put macaroni in large casserole dish, and pour sauce over macaroni. Stir well.

Step 4: Melt butter or margarine in a skillet over medium heat. Add breadcrumbs and brown. Spread over the macaroni and cheese to cover. Sprinkle with a little paprika.

Step 5: Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes. Serve.
